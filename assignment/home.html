Smart Door Lock System

About

Due to COVID, we have to remain contactless as much as possible to reduce the spread of infection. So smart locks use facial recognition system as well as interactive voice module.People who have a 24/7 job or who like to travel a lot would like to keep a track of all the unexpected deliveries as well as guests who arrive at their door steps.A smart virtual assistant will be helpful for conveying messages to specific people such as delivery guys ,guests etc.

Features

  1. Locking/Unlocking door by facial recognition.
  2. Lock/Unlock door at will from any location.
  3. Interactive chatbot.
  4. Electromagnetic lock.
  5. Real-time video footage accessed by admin and a responsive UI.
